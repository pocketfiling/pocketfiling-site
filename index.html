01 · How it works

Three steps. Then emails that make sense.

STEP 01

Add the tickers you track.

Up to 25 on Pro. We watch SEC EDGAR for their next 10-K or 10-Q.

STEP 02

We diff each new filing against the prior one.

The pipeline compares the new filing to the same issuer's last one and extracts new risk factors, removed risk factors, notable shifts in language, and material changes to reported metrics.

STEP 03

You get an email when the diff is ready.

Click through to view the full filing on EDGAR.

02 · Examples

What a typical diff email looks like.

Sample content. Your email uses the actual language from the filing.

SAMPLE
NVDA 10-Q

Guidance reiterated. Supply constraints language tightened. New disclosure on export-license risk.

New risks 2
  • Export-license uncertainty around advanced GPUs to named markets.
  • Increased customer concentration in top 3 accounts.
Removed risks 1
  • Prior-quarter language on component shortages dropped.
Notable shifts 3
  • "Strong demand" to "elevated demand, backlog-driven."
  • Added explicit mention of allocation decisions.
  • Softer tone on near-term pricing power.
Metric changes
Revenue +17% YoY. Gross margin −60bps QoQ. Opex +9% QoQ.
