What counts as a filing diff?

One filing diff = one filing run through our model and turned into a plain-English summary of public SEC filings. If you ask us to summarize Apple's latest 10-K, that's one filing diff. If you add Apple to your watchlist and we send you an alert when it files a new 10-Q, that's also one filing diff.

Re-reading a past filing diff is free and unlimited. We only count the initial run.

How fast are the alerts?

We poll SEC EDGAR continuously. On Pro, your tickers are checked first. Typical end-to-end latency from EDGAR publication to email delivery is well under a minute.

On Free, we run your watchlist every 12 hours. Good enough for position-holders who don't trade on first-print news.

What filings do you cover?

Annual reports (10-K), quarterly reports (10-Q), and material current events (8-K). Coverage spans all ~10,000 US-listed companies that file with the SEC.

What's the refund policy?

Pro has a 7-day refund window from the date of your first paid charge. Email us before day 7 and we refund the full amount, no questions.

After day 7, you can still cancel anytime. You just won't be billed again.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from Settings in one click. No email required, no retention flow. You keep Pro access through the end of the billing period, then drop to Free.

Your watchlist stays intact; we just trim it to 5 tickers (you pick which) if you had more than 5 on Pro.

Is this investment advice?

No. PocketFiling provides a plain-English summary of public SEC filings. We don't suggest trades, rate companies, or issue price targets. We're not a registered investment advisor.

Use the filing diffs to read faster, not to outsource thinking. Always do your own research.

How do I pay?

Stripe, credit or debit card, USD. If your firm needs an invoice for a team setup, email blake@getpocketfiling.com.
